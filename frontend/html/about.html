About Us

Vision & Mission

Mission

  • Provide top-tier education and training in key scientific and technological fields aligned with national economic needs.
  • Engage in applied research to enhance problem-solving capabilities and competitiveness.
  • Act as a center for technology adaptation, innovation, and transfer.
  • Develop essential technical and managerial skills.
  • Establish Ethiopia as a prominent hub for science and technology.

Vision

  • To position Ethiopia as a leading center for science and technology, fostering significant national and international impact.
  • To cultivate a culture of innovation and research that drives sustainable development.
  • To empower future generations with the skills and knowledge necessary for the global marketplace.
  • To collaborate with international partners in advancing scientific and technological initiatives.
  • To ensure access to quality education and resources for all aspiring scientists and technologists.
